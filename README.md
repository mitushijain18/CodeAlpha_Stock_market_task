# CodeAlpha Stock Market & Trading Basics Internship

## Project Overview

This repository contains the tasks and projects completed during the CodeAlpha Stock Market & Trading Basics Internship. The internship provides practical exposure to stock market fundamentals, trading strategies, investment planning, and financial decision-making.

The main objective of this internship is to understand equity markets, analyze stocks, learn trading concepts, manage investment risks, and develop knowledge of financial markets using modern tools and platforms.

## About CodeAlpha Internship

CodeAlpha is a software development company focused on providing students with practical learning opportunities in emerging technologies and professional skills. The Stock Market & Trading Basics Internship helps students understand real-world financial concepts and develop analytical skills required for market analysis and investment planning.

During this internship, various concepts related to stock markets, technical analysis, fundamental analysis, portfolio management, and trading strategies were explored.
