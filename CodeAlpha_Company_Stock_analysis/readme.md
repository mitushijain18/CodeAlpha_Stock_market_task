# Company Stock Analysis Report

## Project Overview

This project is completed as part of the CodeAlpha Stock Market & Trading Basics Internship. The task focuses on analyzing the financial performance and market position of a selected company using fundamental and basic stock analysis techniques.

The objective of this project is to understand how investors evaluate companies by studying financial data, stock performance, business growth, market trends, and investment-related factors.

## About Company Stock Analysis

Company stock analysis is the process of evaluating a company's financial health, market performance, and future growth potential before making investment decisions.

Investors use different analysis methods, such as fundamental analysis and technical analysis, to understand whether a stock is performing well and whether it may provide potential returns.

## Analysis Parameters

### 1. Company Overview

The company profile was studied, including:

* Industry sector

* Market presence

### 2. Fundamental Analysis

Fundamental analysis focuses on evaluating the financial strength and stability of the company.

The following factors were analyzed:

* Revenue growth
* Profitability
* Earnings per share (EPS)
* Market capitalization
* Debt levels
* Return on equity (ROE)
* Dividend history

### 3. Stock Performance Analysis

The stock performance was analyzed using:

* Historical price trends
* Market movements
* Price fluctuations
* Trading volume
* Yearly performance

### 4. Industry Analysis

The company's position within its industry was evaluated based on:

* Market competition
* Industry growth
* Future opportunities
* Market challenges
